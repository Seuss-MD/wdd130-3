About Me

I am a professional graphic designer, photographer, and video editor. I am known for my attention to detail, and my ability to capture moments in time with my photographs and videos. I specialize in business art illustrations and photography. My favorite thing to photograph is candid moments that capture the essence of a person or an event. My favorite things to illustrate are fresh takes on classic iconic characters. I am passionate about capturing real moments of everyday life no matter where they are located or how they happen. I am a graduate of Brigham Young University Idaho.
